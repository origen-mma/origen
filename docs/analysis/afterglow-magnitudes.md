Analysis of 178 O4 gravitational wave events to determine expected afterglow magnitudes for multi-messenger follow-up.

## Full Afterglow Population (Including Off-Axis)

All 178 events produce afterglows, but most are far off-axis and extremely faint:

| Statistic | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Median | 61.3 mag |
| 10th percentile | 26.0 mag |

Off-axis afterglows at typical O4 distances have beaming suppression factors of 100--1000x, making them effectively undetectable.
